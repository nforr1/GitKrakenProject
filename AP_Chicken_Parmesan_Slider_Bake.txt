Chicken Parmesan Slider Bake

Menu Class: Appetizers

Description:
Mini Chicken Parmesan sliders baked together in one tray. Perfect for parties.

Servings: 12

Preparation Time:
Prep 20
Bake 25

Ingredients:
24 ounces frozen breaded chicken tenders
1 package (12 ounces) Hawaiian sweet rolls
14 slices provolone cheese, divided
1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce

Topping:
1/2 cup butter, cubed
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons minced fresh basil

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375Â°. 
2. Prepare chicken tenders according to package directions.
3. Meanwhile, without separating rolls, cut horizontally in half; arrange roll bottoms in a greased 13x9-in. baking dish. 
4. Place 8 cheese slices over roll bottoms, overlapping as needed. Bake until cheese is melted, 3-5 minutes.
5. Layer rolls with half of sauce, chicken tenders, remaining sauce and remaining 6 cheese slices. Replace top halves of rolls.
6. For topping, microwave butter, garlic powder and red pepper flakes, covered, on high, stirring occasionally, until butter is melted.
7. Pour over rolls; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
8. Bake, uncovered, until golden brown and heated through, 20-25 minutes. 
9. Sprinkle with basil before serving.
